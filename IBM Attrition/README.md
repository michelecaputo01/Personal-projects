# IBM Attrition
In this folder an analysis of employee's attrition in IBM is conducted. The dataset has been downloaded for free from [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/yasserh/ibm-attrition-dataset). In particular, the aim is to correctly assign the probability of attrition to each employee according to his departement, his age, his salary, etc. The analysis has been completely carried on using robust and non-parametrical methods, such as innovative aproaches for outlier detection, variable selection and training of Logistic Model and Generalized Additive Model.
